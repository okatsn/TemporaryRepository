Main Research Topics

My research in Earth Sciences focuses on innovative methodologies and analytical techniques to enhance the understanding of soil water content estimation, stochastic earthquake dynamics, and earthquake precursor detection. Below are the main areas of my research:

Stochastic Dynamics and Informational Analysis of Earthquake Rupture Processes

  • Developed and applied stochastic models using the Langevin equation 1 and Fokker-Planck equation 2 to analyze earthquake rupture processes, providing insights into earthquake dynamics and physics-based explanation for empirical scaling laws.
  • Utilized the informational theory 3 approaches to distinguish between different frictional regimes based on both synthetic and experimental data, providing insights into the underlying mechanisms of earthquake process.

  1. Essentially a kind of stochastic differential equation (SDE) that describes the evolution of a system under both deterministic and random forces.

  2. A kind of partial differential equation (PDE) governing the time evolution of the probability density function of a stochastic variable, saying rupture momentum in our case.

  3. Fisher-Shannon analysis

Informational analysis on rock fracture

Fisher Information Measure \[ FIM=\int\limits_{-\infty}^{+\infty}(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}f(x))^2\frac{dx}{f(x)} \]

Shannon Entropy \[ SE=-\int\limits_{-\infty}^{+\infty}f(x)\log f(x) dx \]

Shannon Entropy Power \[ SEP=\frac{1}{2\pi e}e^{2H_X} \]

  • 從右下角的 Langevin Equation 講起

左上角的

  • FIM：根據觀測的分布，度量「估計」的訊息
  • SEP：根據觀測的分布，度量資訊的含量。資訊量越大代表越隨機。

Fitting degree between TIP and target event

Molchan Diagram

Fitting Degree

Alarmed Rate \[ \tau(\textbf{g}) = \frac{\sum_t (TIP(t|\textbf{g}) \equiv 1)}{\sum_t (TIP(t|\textbf{g}) \geq 0)} \]

Missed Rate \[ \nu(\textbf{g}) = \frac{\sum_t(TIP(t|\textbf{g}) \equiv 0 \wedge EQK(t|\textbf{g}) \equiv 1)}{\sum_t(TIP(t|\textbf{g}) \geq 0 \wedge EQK(t|\textbf{g}) \equiv 1)} \]

Fitting Degree \[ D(\textbf{g}) = 1-\tau(\textbf{g})-\nu(\textbf{g}) \]
